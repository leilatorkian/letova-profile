# Add Your Images Here

This folder should contain your portfolio images.

## Required Images (5 total):

### 1. Personal Photo
- **Filename**: `personal-photo.jpg`
- **Purpose**: Your portrait for the intro section
- **Recommended size**: 1200px width
- **Aspect ratio**: Any (will be cropped to fit)

### 2-5. Dress Design Photos
- **Filenames**: 
  - `dress-01.jpg`
  - `dress-02.jpg`
  - `dress-03.jpg`
  - `dress-04.jpg`
- **Purpose**: Your dress designs for the portfolio gallery
- **Recommended size**: 1200px width
- **Aspect ratio**: 3:4 (portrait orientation works best)

## Image Format Support

Supported formats:
- `.jpg` or `.jpeg` (recommended)
- `.png`
- `.webp` (best performance)

## Before Adding Your Images

**Optimize for web:**
1. Resize to recommended dimensions
2. Compress using:
   - ImageOptim (Mac): https://imageoptim.com/
   - Squoosh (Web): https://squoosh.app/
3. Target file size: under 300KB per image

## Quick Start

1. Place all 5 images in this folder with the exact filenames listed above
2. Open `index.html` in your browser to see your portfolio
3. Customize the text content in `index.html` to match your designs

---

**Tip**: If you don't have your images ready yet, you can use placeholder images from:
- https://placeholder.com/
- https://picsum.photos/
- https://unsplash.com/ (free stock photos)

Just make sure to replace them with your actual photos when ready!
