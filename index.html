Leila Torkian

Creating timeless elegance through innovative design — with love and care

Let me introduce you to Letova — a brand that embodies elegance and boldness in every stitch.

With a passion for crafting sophisticated pieces, I specialize in designing garments that blend classic silhouettes with modern sensibilities. Each collection tells a unique story, combining luxurious fabrics with meticulous attention to detail.

My work is inspired by the intersection of art, politics, architecture, and the natural world — resulting in designs that are both contemporary and timeless.

01 May 2023

Fear Into Freedom

Genderless  ·  Womenswear

A wearable manifesto against fear. The feeling of fear opposes freedom — the greater the fear, the further one is from personal liberty. Inspired by the Iranian people's courage in fighting for their rights, this collection visualizes fear and translates it into textile. The color palette — pink, green, and grey — speaks to protest, hope, and exhaustion. Both feminine and unisex, the collection dismantles false gender stereotypes as it dismantles fear itself.

Outfit 3

2023  ·  Reflective Fabric & Felt

Reflective surfaces confront the viewer — a mirror of society's gaze turned back on itself.

Outfit 4

2023  ·  Faux Fur & Wool

Softness as defiance — faux fur textures merge with structured wool to challenge what tenderness means.

Outfit 5

Pink as protest: historically a color of resistance, recast here in clean neoprene lines.

Outfit 6

The closing piece — green for hope, grey for struggle, worn together as one.

Between Worlds

Sportswear  ·  Menswear  ·  Genderless

A study in contrast, asymmetric forms and shifting grey tones inspired by the natural landscapes of Iran, where opposites meet in quiet harmony. The concept references the contrast that exists everywhere — between social levels, genders, in nature, and in different thoughts. Asymmetry of form and a harmonic combination of light and dark shades give shape to this duality.

Outfit 1 – Key Look

2022  ·  Cashmere & Silk

Asymmetric coat in cashmere and wool paired with an asymmetric jumpsuit in cotton and wool — both wearable from either side.

Outfit 2

2022  ·  Cotton & Cashmere

Asymmetric trousers in cotton paired with a hoody featuring two different sleeves, and a cashmere-wool vest fastened with a buttoned waistband.

04 Jun 2020

Worn With Love

Sustainability  ·  Upcycling  ·  Genderless

Upcycling is more than sustainability — it is a philosophy, an emotion, and a way of staying close to the people we love. This collection transforms old, worn garments into something new and modern, beginning with a mother's trenchcoat remade into a fresh silhouette with the same spirit of love and attachment at its core. Two separable pieces, joined by buttons, crafted from reclaimed materials into something made to last.

Outfit 1 – The Trenchcoat

2020  ·  Recycled Cotton & Linen

Remade from a mother's worn trenchcoat into a modern two-piece silhouette. The seams are finished with fabric bands for durability.

06 Jun 2019

Supernormal

Womenswear

Inspired by the everyday object — the Q-Tip. Plain, white, rhythmic in its form when massed together. The dress echoes its shape and color: crafted from a white secondhand bedcover, ironed with unorganized folds to suggest the cotton tips, and organized folds to reflect the rhythmic order of Q-Tips grouped in mass. The commonplace made extraordinary through the logic of repetition.

Outfit 1

2019  ·  Secondhand Bedcover (White Cotton)

A single white secondhand bedcover, ironed into deliberate folds — form and rhythm drawn from Q-Tips grouped in mass.

Wrinkled Thoughts Print
